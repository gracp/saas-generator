# SaaS Generator Tool — Copy Brief

## What is it?
An AI-powered tool that researches markets, generates SaaS ideas, validates them, builds the app, and launches it — end to end.

## Target audience
- Indie hackers wanting to launch micro-SaaS products
- Developers who have ideas but don't want to spend weeks building landing pages
- Entrepreneurs exploring niches before committing
- Side-hustlers who want to test multiple ideas quickly

### 2. How It Works (3 steps)
Step 1: "Pick a niche or let AI find one"
- AI scans Reddit, HN, Product Hunt, Google Trends
- Identifies pain points and underserved markets
- Scores niches by demand, competition, monetization

Step 2: "Generate your SaaS"
- AI creates 5-10 ideas per niche with full specs
- Domain availability check, competitor analysis
- Pick your favorite, customize the MVP scope

Step 3: "Ship it"
- AI generates production-ready Next.js app
- Landing page, auth, Stripe billing, core feature
- Deploys to Vercel with custom domain
- You're live.
